# Changelog

All notable user-facing and release changes are recorded here.

## 1.1.0 - 2026-07-17

- Published an English-first App Store presentation and source release.
- Kept the local-first timeline, optional iCloud sync, and user-configured AI boundaries unchanged.
- Refreshed public screenshots, setup guidance, privacy notes, and release documentation.
- Synchronized the app and Share Extension version at `1.1 (4)`.

## 1.0.0 - 2026-06-28

- First App Store release.
- Local-first text, photo, audio, video, document, comment, check-in, calendar, and search workflows.
- Optional private CloudKit sync across devices on the same Apple Account.
- Optional user-configured AI summaries, titles, topics, and weekly reviews.
- Local archive export and empty-library import.
