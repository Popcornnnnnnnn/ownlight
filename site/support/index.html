To protect your privacy, please avoid sending private journal content, API keys, full export archives, or sensitive media unless you intentionally want me to review them for support.

Common topics

  • privacy: Ownlight is local-first and does not require an account.
  • AI: AI is optional and uses the provider or endpoint you configure.
  • iCloud: iCloud Sync is optional when available and uses your Apple iCloud account.
  • export: Export packages may contain private content and should be stored carefully.

Before contacting support

Please include your app version, iOS version, what you were trying to do, and whether the problem involves recording, media, AI, iCloud, export, or import.
